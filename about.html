The eco-cup Barista is a new coffee shop that has a unique motto to not just to serve it’s customers a cup of coffee but give them a feeling of responsibility towards a sustainable future while consuming their favourite range of beverages. This experience of visiting the coffee shop will give it’s customer a feeling of warmth and belongingness towards the environment.

Our coffee made from hand picked beans from Columbia will give our customers their refreshing dose of the day. We are the only ones in the business with over 150 varities of coffees to choose from.

We also plan to immerse in our employee porfolio recruitng diverse culture and backgrounds, bringing in empathy with customers. Our barista uses only eco-friendly cups and cutlery. We are one of the only coffee shops in North America using solar power for our everyday operations and zero plastic. We cater to large corporate clients, thereby helping them with their corporate responsibility initiatives. We are the official barista partners of retails giants like Walmart and Costco. We are also the official partners of the Toronto International Film Festival. We are also the proud and official sponsors of the Toronto Raptors.
